Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Visionade pricing

How does the free trial work?

You get full access to all Professional plan features for 30 days. No credit card required to start, and you can cancel anytime during the trial period.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards through the Atlassian Marketplace. Enterprise customers can also pay by invoice.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees, ever. Our pricing is transparent with no hidden costs. Enterprise customers get free migration assistance.

What if I need more than 100 users?

Contact our sales team for Enterprise pricing. We offer volume discounts and can customize a plan for your organization.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact support for a full refund.
